Product support

According to the Consumer Protection Act, the primary location for returning a malfunctioning product is the seller’s place of business. However, your feedback is our priority in regard to product development and quality assurance.

At Tammer Brands Ltd, we always process written complaints. This creates fewer misunderstandings and better legal protection for you. If you suspect a material or manufacturing defect in a product, please fill in the form on the Tammer Brands Ltd website.
